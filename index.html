“The teachers really appreciated the option of using Class Chat and many use it on a regular basis for quick reminders to students, etc. They like that the platform is similar to Remind- an app that they have been using for years. Our LMS and Parent Square don't have the same options to reach students on their cellular devices like Class Chat. It is more interactive and the students pay attention to the notifications and check their phones. Announcements on the LMS are often ignored and therefore inefficient. Plus neither eKadence, nor Parent Square allow for a quick and simple back and forth and sharing of pictures, PDFs, etc. between teacher and students, especially teacher and multiple students. Essentially it comes down to the fact that students like the Class Chat format and will use the app.”

Mrs. Brown, Head Principal, Cypress High School
